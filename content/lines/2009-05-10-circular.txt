I like circles
circular motions
revolving doors
planetary rotations
I like circles
ferris wheels
and carousels
such dizzying notions
I like circles
the sun
the moon
and what's in between
the stars I have not yet seen
I like circles
self-referential
circular references
things that end
in the same way
they begin
I like circles
